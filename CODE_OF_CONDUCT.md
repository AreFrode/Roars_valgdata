# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we pledge to make participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include: acting respectfully toward others, being welcoming, and showing empathy.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the maintainers. All reports will be handled with discretion.
